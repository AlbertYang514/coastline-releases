Bundled ONNX Reranker Artifact
-----------------------------

Coastline includes a local ONNX reranker artifact (bundled_models/reranker-v8/)
used only for local candidate-context ranking before user-triggered LLM
analysis. It does not generate text and does not directly write project state.

The bundled artifact is a fine-tuned, ONNX-converted derived model based on:

  Base model:    hfl/rbt3 (Chinese RoBERTa-wwm-ext-small / RBT3)
  Description:   re-trained 3-layer RoBERTa-wwm-ext model
  Publisher:     Joint Laboratory of HIT and iFLYTEK Research (HFL)
  Source:        https://huggingface.co/hfl/rbt3
  License:       Apache-2.0

The ONNX runtime dependency (onnxruntime) and tokenizer library (tokenizers)
are also bundled with the application. See the Backend section above for their
licenses.
